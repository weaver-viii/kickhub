About KickHub

KickHub aims at helping developers to get more donations, and users to donate more wisely.

It is not yet another crowdfunding platform: you give money for what does already exist -- although I plan to allow promising money for stuff things does not exist yet.

KickHub is a free software (check our Github's repo), released under the EPL v1.0 license.
