About KickHub

KickHub aims at helping developers to get more donations, and users to donate more wisely.

It is not yet another crowdfunding platform: you first give money for what does already exist -- although you can use it to publicize your will to donate for features that do not exist yet.

Oh, and KickHub is a free software (check KickHub Github repo), released under the EPL v1.0 license.
